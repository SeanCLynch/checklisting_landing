Checklists should be simple.

Checklists have been used in aviation, construction, surgery, and more with outstanding results. Checklisting Club makes it easy to create, use, share, and iterate on checklists. Checklisting Club is a principled, community-driven project.

Extreme complexity makes it easy to overlook routine matters during pressing circumstances (and moments of forgetfulness!). Formalizing these steps into a well-formatted series of actions, and making it easily accessible in all situations, are the key components of a good checklist. A good checklist, with the proper team culture, can dramatically reduce the damage of careless, trivial errors in a project.
